# 🥓 Vision: The Granola Standard
**Status:** Canonical :: Immutable
**Philosophy:** High-Density, Fact-First, Mechanical.

## 1. The Core Objective
The signal is lost in the noise. Crispy Bacon is not a "tool"; it is a **Mechanical Instrument** for professional reasoning. We reclaim your **Time-to-Think**. We do not "summarize"; we distill raw capture into high-density strategic nodes, mirroring the skeletal cognitive structure of a master analyst.

## 2. The Design Principles
- **Crunchy (Tactile Clarity):** Interfaces must be physical. We use sharp borders, heavy black headers, and deep layered shadows. Every interaction must feel like a mechanical switch.
- **Dense (Signal > Noise):** We banish conversational filler ("In this meeting..."). We prioritize objective bullet points, hard constraints, and structured decisions.
- **Organic (Human Language):** We avoid "AI," "LLM," and "Ingestion." We use **"Reasoning," "Recap,"** and **"Vault."** The assistant is a partner, not a script.

## 3. The No-Bot Standard
We respect the social fabric of professional calls.
- **Direct Loopback:** Bacon captures audio from the system hardware directly.
- **Invisible Intelligence:** We do not send bots into meetings. No extra avatars, no recording notifications, no interruption of flow.

## 4. Truth-First Reasoning
Our assistant is prohibited from guessing. If a decision wasn't made, the system reports **"No decision detected."** We prioritize **Accuracy over Politeness.** The "Strategic Trace" ensures every highlight is grounded in the original signal.

## 5. Performance Aesthetics
- **Sandstone:** Low-strain background for daylight operations.
- **Charcoal:** Stratified depth for high-focus night work.
- **Newsreader:** Serif typography for long-form comprehension.
- **JetBrains Mono:** Monospace for data density and metadata.
