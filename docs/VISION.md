# 🥓 Vision: The Granola Standard
**Status:** Canonical :: Product Manifesto
**Philosophy:** High-Density. Zero Jargon. Quiet Power.

## 1. The Core Objective
Crispy Bacon is not a "platform." It is a quiet place to think.

It is a professional instrument designed to recover lost information from meetings and documents. We do not "optimize workflows" or "leverage synergies." We **clarify thinking**.

Our goal is to reduce cognitive load. Every pixel and every word must fight against complexity.

---

## 2. Voice & Tone: Human-Grade
We speak like a senior executive assistant: concise, professional, and calm. We never speak like a sci-fi computer or a military commander.

### ❌ The Ban List (Automatic Rejection)
*   **No Military/Ops Terms:** *Protocol, Vector, Deployment, Operation, Command, Extraction, Telemetry.*
*   **No Dev-Speak:** *Initialize, array, hash, latency, buffer, stack, node, ingest.*
*   **No "Bro-Tech":** *Crush, dominate, leverage, 10x, beast mode, unlock potential.*

### ✅ The Approved Vocabulary
*   **Actions:** *Start, Stop, Save, Import, Record, Ask.*
*   **Objects:** *Note, Library, Folder, Tag, Recording, Summary.*
*   **States:** *Working, Ready, Saved, Private.*
*   **Concepts:** *Clarity, Focus, Privacy, Accuracy.*

**Rule of Thumb:** If you wouldn't say it to a CEO in a quiet room, don't put it in the interface.

---

## 3. The "No-Bot" Standard
We respect the social fabric of professional work.
*   **Invisible:** We capture audio from the system hardware (loopback). We **never** send "Participant Bots" into Zoom/Teams calls.
*   **Private:** No avatars. No "AI is listening" announcements. No disruption. The user is in complete control.

---

## 4. Intelligence: Facts Over Fluff
*   **Skeletal Intelligence:** Summaries must be dense. Strip away "In this meeting, the speakers discussed..." and go straight to the bullet points.
*   **Radical Honesty:** If a decision wasn't made, say "No decision detected." Do not hallucinate a conclusion just to be helpful.
*   **Evidence:** Every claim in a summary must be traceable.

---

## 5. Visual Design: "Granola"
The interface is tactile, grounded, and organic. It should feel like a physical object.
*   **Sandstone & Charcoal:** Natural, low-strain colors. No neon.
*   **Hard Lines:** Sharp, distinct 1px borders. Elements are cards, not floating gradients.
*   **Typography:**
    *   *Newsreader (Serif)* for reading.
    *   *Inter (Sans)* for controls.
    *   *JetBrains Mono* for data.
