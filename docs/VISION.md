# 🥓 Vision & Philosophy: The Organic Standard
**Status:** Canonical :: Lead Architect Approved

## 1. The Core Objective
Professionals are drowning in noise. **Crispy Bacon** exists to create **Clarity**. We don't just "process data"; we turn messy conversations into clear, thoughtful notes that read like they were written by a human partner.

## 2. The Organic Principles
* **Warmth (Humanist Design):** The app is an instrument, not a machine. We use warm "Pampas" backgrounds, "Newsreader" serif headers, and tactile rust-orange accents. It should feel like a fresh sheet of premium paper.
* **Plain Spoken (Human Language):** We reject "Tech-Bro" jargon. There is no "Ingestion," "Vectors," or "Deploying." We simply "Record," "Read," and "Save." If your mom wouldn't say it, the UI shouldn't say it.
* **Dense (Signal > Noise):** Clarity comes from brevity. We strip away the fluff ("In this meeting...") to reveal the core decisions. We respect the user's time by being concise, not chatty.

## 3. The No-Bot Standard
We respect the social fabric of conversation.

* **Direct Listening:** We record directly from your browser tab or microphone.
* **Invisible Presence:** We never send "AI Bots" to join your calls. No avatars, no beeps, no interruptions. We are a silent note-taker.

## 4. Truth-First Reasoning
Our notes are honest. If a decision wasn't made, we say "No decision detected." We prioritize **Accuracy over Politeness**. We cite our sources so you can trust what you read.

## 5. Performance Aesthetics
* **Sandstone & Charcoal:** Low-strain palettes for deep work.
* **Newsreader Serif:** Editorial typography for reading long notes comfortably.
* **Geist Mono:** A clean, subtle font for timestamps and IDs (used sparingly).
