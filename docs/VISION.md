
# 🥓 Vision & Philosophy: The Organic Standard
**Status:** Canonical :: Lead Architect Approved

## 1. The Core Objective
Professionals are drowning in meeting noise. **Crispy Bacon** exists to build **Time-to-Think**. We don't just "process data"; we transform raw information into high-clarity strategic notes that mirror the cognitive structure of a top-tier analyst.

## 2. The Organic Principles
* **Warmth (Humanist Design):** Interfaces should feel like a premium notebook, not a command terminal. We use warm cream backgrounds, elegant serif headers, and tactile rust-orange accents. The app is a quiet place for deep thought.
* **Dense (Signal > Noise):** No AI fluff. We banish "In this meeting, the team discussed..." and replace it with direct facts. We prefer objective bullet points and structured decisions over long paragraphs.
* **Natural (Human Language):** We avoid jargon like "Ingestion," "Telemetry," or "Latency." We use "Import," "Activity," and "Speed." The assistant is a partner, not a utility.

## 3. The No-Bot Standard
We respect the social fabric of professional calls.

* **Direct Recording:** Bacon captures audio from your system directly.
* **Invisible Intelligence:** We do not send bots into meetings. No extra avatars, no intrusive recording warnings, no interruption of the human flow.

## 4. Truth-First Reasoning
Our assistant is honest. If a decision wasn't made, the assistant says "No decision detected." We prioritize **Accuracy over Politeness**. The "Strategic Trace" ensures every highlight is grounded in the original source.

## 5. Performance Aesthetics
* **Sandstone & Charcoal:** A low-strain, warm palette designed for long reading sessions.
* **Newsreader Serif:** Used for headers to evoke the authority of editorial print.
* **Geist Mono:** A clean, professional monospace for data density without the "hacker" aesthetic.
